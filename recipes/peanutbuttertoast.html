Peanut Butter Toast

Description

Peanut butter toast is a simple and delicious meal anyone can make at home with just a few ingredients

Steps

  1. Get Slices of bread
  2. Get peanut butter and knife
  3. Put toast into the toaster and toast until golden brown
  4. Spread layer of peanut butter onto toast

Thats it! You now have a great and easy recipe that works for a great snack any time of the day.
